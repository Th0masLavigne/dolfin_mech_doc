dolfin_mech.Material_Elastic_OgdenCiarletGeymonatNeoHookean

class OgdenCiarletGeymonatNeoHookeanElasticMaterial(kinematics, parameters, decoup=False)[source]

Bases: ElasticMaterial

Class representing a composite hyperelastic material combining the Ciarlet-Geymonat and Neo-Hookean models.

This model provides a robust description of compressible hyperelasticity by additive decomposition of the strain energy density:

\[\Psi_{total} = \Psi_{bulk}(J) + \Psi_{dev}(\mathbf{C})\]
Where:

  • \(\Psi_{bulk}\) is the Ciarlet-Geymonat potential (penalizing volume change).

  • \(\Psi_{dev}\) is the Neo-Hookean potential (governing shear response).

Beyond standard stress tensors, this class computes the hydrostatic pressure and the Von Mises equivalent of the Second Piola-Kirchhoff stress.
